About youTHrive Malta

Discover our story, our team, and our mission to empower people through innovation and inclusion.

Who We Are

youTHrive is a forward-thinking organization dedicated to empowering people by unlocking their potential through innovative learning experiences. With extensive training experience, we understand the importance of a tailored approach to meet unique needs related to disadvantaged backgrounds.

Our programs blend fun techniques like animated videos, vibrant graphics, and gamification to make learning enjoyable and ensure real-world application. Our expertise in digital skills, labour market skills, finance education, and teacher training allows us to deliver impactful programs that resonate with today's reality.

We are committed to creating inclusive learning environments, particularly for those on the autism spectrum and with ADHD. In Malta's multicultural landscape, we work closely with other adult trainers, schools, VET and HEIs to implement tools that support diverse communities.

Meet Our Team

The dedicated professionals behind youTHrive Malta

Kevin Spiteri

Managing Director

Kevin has extensive experience in training youth and adult workers, course evaluation, and educational consultancy. He specializes in addressing the challenges faced by educators and young learners as they adapt to the digital age. Kevin leads the development of training tools and professional development programs for adult trainers, bringing innovative ideas and best practices to support youth workers in developing essential skills for the evolving labour market.

Marika Huber

Project Manager

Marika brings extensive experience in European project management, leadership, and financial management, particularly within the Erasmus program. She has worked on projects related to woman empowerment in the workplace and is currently participating in two Erasmus adult projects focused on women equality in the workplace. As a former Managing Director of the Mediterranean Bank Network, Marika forged significant relationships within the sector to support social entrepreneurship. She holds an MBA from Maastricht, earned in 2003.
