YOUTHCENTS – Financial Literacy for Youth

YOUTHCENTS is a European project co-funded by Erasmus+, designed to empower young people aged 15–26 with essential financial literacy and entrepreneurship skills.

  • Combines innovative digital tools, animated videos, and non-formal learning methods
  • Helps youth—especially those from disadvantaged or neurodiverse backgrounds—gain confidence and knowledge to manage finances
  • Encourages planning for the future and exploring social entrepreneurship

Supporting Youth Workers & Promoting Inclusion

The project also supports youth workers through tailored training resources, fostering inclusive and impactful mentorship.
Led by partners in Portugal, Malta, and Poland, YOUTHCENTS promotes financial inclusion, sustainable thinking, and youth employability across Europe.
