No MOOCs

Please note: youTHrive does not offer MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) as part of our training portfolio, in line with our PIF statement. We focus on interactive, tailored, and high-quality learning experiences.

Our Approach

youTHrive Malta is dedicated to empowering people through innovative learning experiences. Our expertise spans digital skills, labour market readiness, finance education, and teacher training. We are committed to inclusivity, working with diverse communities and focusing on real-world impact. Our team includes experienced trainers and quality evaluators, ensuring every program is impactful and relevant.

We do not send people overseas for mobilities, but we are open to organizing training events in Malta. Our travel is limited to one person, max three trips per project, and only for up to two nights.
